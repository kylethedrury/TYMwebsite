We are TeamYouthMusic!

TYM is a university student-led initiative established in Brampton, Ontario that aims to bring musical literacy and fluency back to the forefront of modern education. We are experienced and dedicated to mentoring the next generation of musicians and are united by this objective. We currently offer piano and violin lessons.

Our Teachers

We follow the curriculum set by the internationally recognized Royal Conservatory of Music (not affiliated), the gold standard of music education. Our teachers are young, high-level RCM students who have been learning for years, and are looking to expand their repertoire of experience by instilling their passion for music into others. If you are an RCM student and this sounds like you, we encourage you to get in touch with us below!
